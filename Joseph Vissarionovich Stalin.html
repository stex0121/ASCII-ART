Stalin’s quotes reveal the core of his ideology, built on total control, political pragmatism, and a totalitarian view of the world.

He believed that truth was not universal, but something dictated by the Party —

“it doesn’t matter what people think, only what we make them think.

” To him, art and literature were tools of ideology,

as “writers are the engineers of the human soul.” Morality held no value in his system, since “no man —

no problem,” and any crime was justified if it served the revolution. The system thrived on paranoia and the constant hunt for enemies —

“those we don’t have are our enemies,” because ideological loyalty mattered more than truth or

justice. The Party was a godlike entity, and bureaucracy held ultimate power — “one signature is stronger than ten divisions.”

He saw democracy as deception, claiming “democracy is just a decoration for capitalism.” All these statements reflect an

ideological system where the end justifies any means, where the individual is disposable,

loyalty to the regime is absolute, and the world is eternally divided between “us” and “them,” leaving no room for neutrality or dialogue.





Staljinovi citati otkrivaju srž njegove ideologije, zasnovane na potpunoj kontroli, političkom pragmatizmu i totalitarnom pogledu na svet.

Verovao je da istina nije univerzalna, već nešto što diktira Partija —

„nije važno šta ljudi misle, već šta ih mi nateramo da misle.“

Za njega su umetnost i književnost bili alati ideologije,

jer su „pisci inženjeri ljudske duše.“ Moral nije imao vrednost u njegovom sistemu, jer „nema čoveka —

nema problema,“ a svaki zločin bio je opravdan ako je služio revoluciji. Sistem je počivao na paranoji i stalnoj potrazi za neprijateljima —

„oni koje nemamo su naši neprijatelji,“ jer je ideološka lojalnost bila važnija od istine ili

pravde. Partija je bila božansko biće, a birokratija je imala krajnju moć — „jedan potpis je jači od deset divizija.“

Demokratiju je smatrao obmanom, tvrdeći da je „demokratija samo dekoracija za kapitalizam.“ Sve ove izjave odražavaju

ideološki sistem u kojem cilj opravdava svako sredstvo, gde je pojedinac potrošan,

lojalnost režimu apsolutna, a svet zauvek podeljen između „nas“ i „njih,“ bez prostora za neutralnost ili dijalog.
