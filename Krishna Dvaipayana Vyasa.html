Krishna Dvaipayana Veda Vyasa

“Dharma protects those who protect dharma.”

“Time creates all things, and time destroys them all.”

“In battle, victory is not always with the righteous.”

“The mind is everything. It makes heaven of hell and hell of heaven.”

“You have the right to perform your duty, but not to the fruits of action.”

“Desire and anger are the enemies of the soul.”

“A man is made by his faith. As he believes, so he becomes.”

“When meditation is mastered, the mind is unwavering like the flame of a lamp in a windless place.”

“He who sees inaction in action, and action in inaction, is wise among men.”

“From anger comes delusion; from delusion, loss of memory; from loss of memory, destruction of intelligence; and from destruction of intelligence, one perishes.”
